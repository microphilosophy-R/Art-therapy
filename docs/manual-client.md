## 1. Welcome

Welcome to the Art Therapy Platform. This manual will guide you through everything you need to know as a client, from creating your account to booking sessions, joining group events, and communicating with your therapist.

This platform connects you with licensed art therapists for private one-on-one sessions and group therapy events. All sessions can be attended in person or online, depending on what the therapist offers and what you choose at the time of booking.

You do not need any technical experience to use this platform. Simply follow the steps in this manual and you will be able to find a therapist and book your first session with confidence.

---

## 2. Creating Your Account

Before you can book a session or browse therapists, you need to register for a free account.

**Steps to register:**

1. Go to the platform's website and click the **Sign Up** or **Register** button on the homepage.
2. Fill in the registration form with the following information:
   - **First name** and **last name**
   - **Email address** (this will be your login username)
   - **Password** (choose something secure that you will remember)
3. When asked to select your role, choose **Client**. This is important — selecting the wrong role will affect what you can see and do on the platform.
4. Click **Create Account** (or similar button) to submit your registration.
5. You may receive a confirmation email. If so, open the email and click the confirmation link to activate your account.

Once your account is active, you can log in and begin using the platform.

---

## 3. Logging In and Out

### Logging In

1. Go to the platform's website.
2. Click the **Log In** button.
3. Enter your **email address** and **password**.
4. Click **Log In**.

If you have forgotten your password, look for a **Forgot Password** or **Reset Password** link on the login page. You will be sent an email with instructions to set a new password.

### Logging Out

To log out, look for your name or profile picture in the top-right corner of the screen. Click on it to open a small menu, then click **Log Out**. It is a good habit to log out whenever you are using a shared or public computer.

---

## 4. Navigating the Platform

Once you are logged in, you will see a navigation bar at the top of every page. This bar contains links to the main areas of the platform:

- **Home** — returns to the homepage
- **Therapists** — browse and search for therapists
- **Therapy Plans** — browse group events open for sign-up
- **Gallery** — view completed therapy programmes
- **Dashboard** — your personal area: appointments, messages, and forms
- **Profile** — your account settings
- **Language switcher** — switch between English and Chinese (Mandarin)
- **Notification bell** — shows alerts and messages

---

## 5. Changing the Language

The platform is available in **English** and **Chinese (Mandarin)**.

To switch languages, find the language switcher in the navigation bar at the top of the screen. It will show either **EN** (for English) or **中文** (for Chinese). Click it to switch to the other language.

The change takes effect immediately and applies to all pages across the platform. Your language preference is saved, so the platform will remember your choice the next time you visit.

## 6. Finding a Therapist

### Browsing the Therapist Directory

Go to the **Therapists** page (you can click **Therapists** in the navigation bar). You will see a list of therapist cards. Each card shows:

- The therapist's name and photo
- Their specialties (for example: anxiety, grief, trauma, creative expression)
- Their city or location
- Their rating from previous clients
- Their session price

Browse through the cards to find a therapist who seems like a good fit for you.

### Viewing a Therapist's Full Profile

Click on a therapist's card to open their full profile page. Here you will find:

- A detailed biography and background
- Their areas of specialty
- Reviews and ratings from other clients
- Their session price in Chinese Yuan (CNY)
- If you are based outside of China, an approximate equivalent price in US Dollars (USD) will also be shown for your reference

Take your time reading through profiles. Choosing the right therapist is an important step.

### Booking from a Therapist's Profile

When you are ready to book a session with a therapist, click the **Book a Session** button on their profile page. This will take you through the booking process, which is described in the next section.

## 7. Booking a Private Session

Booking a private, one-on-one session is done in several steps. You will move through each step one at a time, and you can review everything before you pay.

### Step 1: Acknowledgement and Terms

Before proceeding, you must review and acknowledge the **Terms of Service** specific to the service you are booking. 
- Read the key points regarding privacy, cancellations, and session rules.
- Check the box "I have read and agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy."
- Click **Next** to proceed.

### Step 2: Date, Time, and Duration

You will see a calendar view showing the therapist's availability.
- **Select a Date:** Click on a highlighted day to see available slots.
- **Select a Time:** Choose a specific time slot from the list.
- **Choose Duration:** Use the duration dropdown to select how long you want the session to be (e.g., 60, 90, 120 minutes).

If you are not sure which date to pick, click the **Find Next Available** button to skip to the nearest opening. Click **Next** to continue.

### Step 3: Add Additional Information (Optional)

In this step, you can provide any extra details that might be helpful for the therapist.
- **Notes/Requests:** Briefly describe your goals or any background information (maximum 1,000 characters).
- **Invite a Friend (Coming Soon):** You will see an option to invite a friend for collaborative sessions or discounts in the future.
- **Note:** This step is automatically skipped for certain group events like Art Salons where additional information is not required.

Click **Next** when you are ready.

### Step 4: Final Check and Payment Method

Review the summary of your booking:
- **Service Details:** Check the date, time, and session format.
- **Price:** Confirm the total amount.
- **Select Payment Method:** Choose between **Alipay**, **WeChat Pay**, or **Waitlist/Card** (if available).

### Step 5: Complete Payment & Confirmation

Once you click **Confirm and Pay**, you will be guided through the specific payment process (scanning a QR code or being redirected). After successful payment:
- You will see a **Success** page.
- You will be redirected to your **Dashboard**.
- Your appointment will show as **PENDING** until the therapist accepts it.

## 8. Payment Methods

### Paying by Card (Credit or Debit)

When you select **Card** as your payment method, a secure card payment form will appear on screen. Enter the following details:

- Card number
- Card expiry date
- Security code (the 3 or 4 digit code on your card)
- Cardholder name (as it appears on your card)

Click **Pay** to complete the transaction. Your card details are handled securely by Stripe, a trusted international payment provider. The platform itself does not store your card information.

### Paying by Alipay

When you select **Alipay**, you will be redirected to the Alipay website or app to complete your payment. Follow the prompts within Alipay, and once the payment is confirmed you will be returned to the platform and shown your booking confirmation.

### Paying by WeChat Pay

When you select **WeChat Pay**, a QR code will appear on your screen. Open your WeChat app on your phone, tap the scan icon, and scan the QR code. Confirm the payment within WeChat. Once the payment goes through, the platform will automatically detect it and show you your booking confirmation.

## 9. After You Book: Understanding Appointment Statuses

Every appointment has a status label so you always know what stage it is at. Here is what each status means:

| Status | What it means |
|---|---|
| **PENDING** | Your booking has been received and is waiting for the therapist to accept it. |
| **CONFIRMED** | The therapist has accepted your booking. Your session is confirmed. |
| **IN PROGRESS** | Your session is currently underway. |
| **COMPLETED** | Your session has finished. |
| **CANCELLED** | The session has been cancelled, either by you, by the therapist, or automatically by the system. |

### Automatic Cancellation

If your therapist has not accepted your booking by 24 hours before the scheduled start time, the appointment will be automatically cancelled by the platform. You will receive a notification to let you know, and a full refund will be issued. You are then free to book with the same therapist at a different time, or to choose a different therapist.

## 10. Your Client Dashboard

Your Dashboard is your personal home base on the platform. To reach it, click **Dashboard** in the navigation bar.

Your Dashboard is organised into tabs:

### Upcoming Appointments

This tab shows all your future sessions. Each appointment displays:

- The therapist's name
- The date and time
- The session format (video or in-person)
- The current status (for example, PENDING or CONFIRMED)

### Past Appointments

This tab shows sessions that have already taken place or been completed. You can use this as a record of your therapy history.

### Messages

This tab shows all your notifications and messages, including:

- Confirmation that your booking has been accepted
- Alerts if an appointment is cancelled
- Notifications when a therapist sends you a form to fill in
- Updates about group events you have signed up for

### Forms

This tab shows any intake or assessment forms your therapist has sent you. See Section 14 for full instructions on completing forms.

## 11. Cancelling an Appointment

If you need to cancel an appointment, you can do so from your Dashboard.

**Steps to cancel:**

1. Go to your **Dashboard**.
2. Find the appointment you wish to cancel in the **Upcoming Appointments** tab.
3. Click the **Cancel** button next to that appointment.
4. You will be asked to confirm that you want to cancel. Confirm to proceed.

The **Cancel** button is only available for appointments with a status of **PENDING** or **CONFIRMED**. You cannot cancel an appointment that is already in progress or completed.

### Refunds When You Cancel

Whether you receive a refund depends on the individual therapist's cancellation policy, which is displayed on the Review step before you pay. As a general guide, most therapists offer a full refund if you cancel at least 24 hours before your session. If you cancel with less than 24 hours' notice, a partial refund or no refund may apply.

Always check the refund policy shown on the booking review page before completing your payment so that you are aware of the terms.

## 12. Group Events and Therapy Plans

In addition to private sessions, the platform offers group events that you can join. These are found on the **Therapy Plans** page (click **Therapy Plans** in the navigation bar).

### Types of Group Events

There are three types of group events:

- **Group Consultation** — a small group session led by a therapist, focused on a specific theme or therapeutic goal
- **Art Salon** — an open and relaxed creative session where participants explore art-making together
- **Wellness Retreat** — a multi-day, immersive programme combining therapy and wellbeing activities

### Browsing Therapy Plans

On the Therapy Plans page, you will see cards for all published events. Each card shows the event name, type, and key details.

Click on a plan to open its detail page. Here you will find:

- A full description of the event
- The schedule (dates and times)
- The location (or whether it is held online)
- The maximum number of participants
- How many people have already signed up
- The price per seat (in CNY)
- The current status of the plan

### Plan Status Labels

| Status | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Published** | The event is open and you can sign up. |
| **Sign-ups Closed** | The registration period has ended. New sign-ups are no longer accepted. |
| **In Progress** | The event is currently underway. |
| **Finished** | The event has ended. |
| **In Gallery** | The event has been archived and can be viewed in the Gallery. |

### Signing Up for a Group Event

If a plan is **Published** and you have not already signed up, you will see a **Sign Up** button showing the price.

**Steps to sign up:**

1. Click the **Sign Up** button on the plan detail page.
2. The **Standard Payment Workflow** will open.
3. **Acknowledge Terms:** Review and accept the group-specific terms of service.
4. **Confirm Schedule:** Review the fixed dates and times for the event. Note: The "Optional Information" page is automatically skipped for most group plans.
5. **Payment:** Choose **Alipay**, **WeChat Pay**, or **Card** and complete the transaction.

After signing up, the plan detail page will show an **Enrolled** badge to confirm your place has been secured.

---

## 13. Cancelling a Group Sign-Up

If you need to cancel your sign-up for a group event, this is only possible while the plan still has a status of **Published** (that is, before the sign-up period closes).

**Steps to cancel a group sign-up:**

1. Go to the plan detail page for the event you signed up for.
2. If cancellation is available, you will see a **Cancel** button.
3. Click **Cancel** and confirm your decision.

Once sign-ups have closed, or the event has moved to any other status, cancellation from within the platform is no longer possible. If you have an urgent situation, please contact the platform's support team or your therapist directly.

## 14. Filling In Forms

Your therapist may ask you to complete a form before or during your time working together. These are typically intake questionnaires (to help your therapist understand your background and goals) or ongoing assessment forms (to track your progress).

### How to Know When You Have a Form

When your therapist sends you a form, you will receive a notification. The notification bell icon in the top navigation bar will show a number indicating you have unread notifications. You will also see a message in your Dashboard under the **Messages** tab.

### How to Complete a Form

1. Go to your **Dashboard**.
2. Click on the **Forms** tab.
3. You will see a list of forms assigned to you. Click on the form you wish to complete.
4. Read each question carefully and provide your answer. Forms may include:
   - **Text fields** — type your written answer
   - **Multiple choice** — select one or more options from a list
   - **Rating scales** — choose a number on a scale (for example, 1 to 5 or 1 to 10)
   - **Yes/No questions** — simply select Yes or No
5. Make sure to answer all required questions (these are usually marked with an asterisk or marked as required).
6. When you have answered all questions, click **Submit**.

Once submitted, your therapist will be able to view your responses. You will not usually be able to edit your answers after submitting, so take your time and answer thoughtfully.

## 15. Messages and Notifications

### The Notification Bell

The bell icon in the top navigation bar is your notification centre. When you have unread notifications, a small number will appear on the bell icon showing how many new alerts you have.

Click the bell icon to see a brief list of recent notifications. Click on a notification to go directly to the relevant page.

### Types of Notifications You Will Receive

The platform will automatically send you notifications when:

- A therapist **confirms** your appointment
- An appointment is **cancelled** (by you, the therapist, or automatically)
- Your therapist **sends you a form** to complete
- A **group event** you signed up for is starting or has finished
- Your sign-up for a group event is **confirmed**

### Viewing All Messages

To see all your messages and notifications in full, go to your **Dashboard** and click the **Messages** tab. This gives you a complete history of all your notifications in one place.

## 16. The Gallery

The Gallery is a showcase of completed therapy programmes and artwork from past group events. You can access it by clicking **Gallery** in the navigation bar.

Browsing the Gallery is a good way to get a sense of what programmes have been run in the past, the kinds of artwork and creative work that participants have produced, and what you might experience in a group event.

The Gallery does not require you to be logged in — it is open to anyone visiting the platform.

## 17. Managing Your Profile

Your profile page is where you can update your personal information and change your password. To reach it, click your name or profile picture in the navigation bar and select **Profile**, or click **Profile** if it appears directly in the navigation bar.

### Updating Your Personal Information

On your profile page, you can update:

- **First name and last name**
- **Phone number** — useful if your therapist needs to contact you
- **Profile photo** — click the photo area or the upload button to choose an image from your device

After making your changes, click the **Save** or **Update** button to apply them.

### Changing Your Password

On your profile page, find the **Change Password** section.

1. Enter your **current password**.
2. Enter your **new password**.
3. Enter your **new password again** to confirm it.
4. Click **Save** or **Update Password**.

Choose a password that is at least 8 characters long and includes a mix of letters and numbers. Do not share your password with anyone.

### Privacy Consent Settings

Your profile page also includes a section for **Privacy Consent**. Here you can review and update your preferences regarding how your information is used on the platform. Read the options carefully and adjust them according to your preferences.

## 18. Frequently Asked Questions

**What happens if I book a session and the therapist does not confirm it?**

If your therapist does not accept your booking at least 24 hours before the scheduled session time, the appointment is automatically cancelled. You will receive a notification, and a full refund will be returned to your original payment method.

**Can I change the date or time of my booking after I have paid?**

Currently the platform does not offer a rescheduling option. If you need to change the time, you would need to cancel your existing booking (subject to the refund policy) and make a new booking at a different time. Contact your therapist if you need assistance with this.

**Is my payment information stored on the platform?**

No. Card payments are handled entirely by Stripe, a secure and internationally trusted payment processor. The platform does not see or store your full card details. Alipay and WeChat Pay payments are handled by their respective services.

**What if I want to switch therapists?**

You are free to book with any therapist on the platform at any time. Simply go to the Therapists page and browse for a different therapist. There is no requirement to stay with one therapist.

**Can I attend a group event and also book private sessions with the same therapist?**

Yes. Group events and private sessions are independent of each other. You can sign up for a group event run by a therapist while also booking individual sessions with them.

**What if I have a technical problem or cannot access the platform?**

If you experience any difficulty using the platform, please contact the support team using the contact details provided on the platform's website. When contacting support, it is helpful to describe what you were trying to do, what happened, and what device and browser you were using.

**Can I use the platform on my phone?**

Yes. The platform is designed to work on both computers and mobile devices. You can access it through the web browser on your phone or tablet.

**How do I know if my session is video or in-person?**

The session format is shown on your booking confirmation page and in your Dashboard under Upcoming Appointments. You choose the format yourself during the booking process.

**What do I need for a video session?**

For a video session, you will need a device with a camera and microphone (such as a laptop, tablet, or smartphone) and a stable internet connection. Your therapist will provide details on how to join the video call closer to your session time.

**Can I use the platform in Chinese?**

Yes. The platform supports both English and Mandarin Chinese. Use the language switcher in the navigation bar to change the language at any time.
